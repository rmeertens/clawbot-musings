# Half-Day City Tour: Amsterdam

**Duration:** ~4 hours  
**Best time:** Morning (start 9:00) or afternoon (start 13:00)  
**Transport:** Walking and tram

## Overview

Amsterdam's historic canal ring is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. This tour takes you through the Golden Age merchant neighbourhoods, a world-class museum, and the lively Jordaan district — all within half a day.

---

## Route

### 1. Rijksmuseum (1.5 hours)
Start at the **Rijksmuseum** on Museumplein. Home to Rembrandt's *Night Watch* and Vermeer's *The Milkmaid*, the collection spans Dutch Golden Age art and history. Buy tickets online in advance to skip the queue.

> **Tip:** Enter through the tunnel passage even if you skip the museum — it's free and beautifully decorated.

### 2. Vondelpark (20 minutes)
Walk north to **Vondelpark**, Amsterdam's beloved green lung. Stroll along the main path toward the rose garden. Grab a coffee at the open-air café **Blauwe Theehuis** (the Blue Teahouse, shaped like a flying saucer).

### 3. Nine Streets (De Negen Straatjes) (30 minutes)
Head into the canal belt via the **Nine Streets** — a grid of charming shopping alleys between the main canals. Browse independent boutiques, vintage shops, and bakeries. Cross the Prinsengracht, Keizersgracht, and Herengracht canals.

### 4. Anne Frank House area & Westerkerk (20 minutes)
Walk along the **Prinsengracht** to the **Westerkerk**, the tallest church in Amsterdam. Admire the view of the canal from the church square. The **Anne Frank House** is directly across the canal — book well ahead if you want to visit.

### 5. Jordaan neighbourhood (45 minutes)
Finish in the **Jordaan**, Amsterdam's most atmospheric quarter. Wander down **Bloemgracht** (Flower Canal), peek into the **Begijnhof-like hofjes** (hidden courtyards), and end at a brown café (*bruin café*) for a local *pils* or *jenever* (Dutch gin).

## Getting There

From Amsterdam Centraal Station, take tram 2 or 12 to **Museumplein** (10 minutes). The station is well connected to Schiphol Airport (20 min by train).
