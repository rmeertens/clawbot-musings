## History & Fun Facts

Use bullet points. Each one should be a genuinely surprising fact, not a Wikipedia lead paragraph. Aim for:
- Superlatives ("the only / the first / the tallest / the longest")
- Unexpected connections (famous person born here, treaty signed here, invention made here)
- Numbers that land ("destroyed 85% of the city in 15 minutes")
- Something the visitor can look for in person

## Format Notes for Contributors

1. **Voice:** Write like a knowledgeable friend who has been there recently, not a travel agent. Use "you'll see", "look for", "most people miss this".
2. **Opinions:** Be specific. Don't say "a great café" — say which one and why.
3. **Stop transitions:** Always include a "walk to next stop" paragraph with actual directions and something to look at on the way.
4. **Time budgets:** Be realistic. Include queue time for popular museums. Note what to skip if running late.
5. **Language:** The NL version is a full translation, not a word-for-word copy — use natural Dutch phrasing.
6. **File naming:** `city-name.md` in `en/` and `nl/` folders. Use hyphens, lowercase (e.g. `den-haag.md`).
7. **Frontmatter:** Always include `layout`, `lang`, `city_slug`, `title`, `description`.
